SECTION 1: BATTLE OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The virus battle is a real-time territorial conflict between 2-4 viruses on a 
64x40 grid. Each virus starts from a corner and spreads across the grid, 
converting cells to their color. The battle is driven by 12 parameters that 
players distribute 12 points across before the battle begins.

BATTLE DURATION: 60-120 seconds (average)
GRID SIZE: 64 x 40 = 2,560 cells
WIN CONDITION: Control 80% of grid OR have most territory when time expires

================================================================================
SECTION 2: THE 12 PARAMETERS
================================================================================

Each virus has 12 parameters. Players distribute exactly 12 points across them.
No parameter can exceed 10 points.

SECTION 3: BATTLE PHASES
================================================================================

PHASE 1: EXPANSION (0-30% grid coverage)
─────────────────────────────────────────
• No contact between viruses yet
• Primary parameters: SPEED, REPRODUCTION, STEALTH
• Visual: Clean territory edges, smooth growth animation
• Mechanics:
  - Each virus expands from its corner
  - Empty cells convert to virus color based on REPRODUCTION stat
  - SPEED determines animation playback rate
  - STEALTH keeps virus hidden from enemy view until contact

PHASE 2: DETECTION (30-50% grid coverage)
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Viruses approach each other
• Stealth check occurs
• Primary parameters: STEALTH, INTELLECT
• Visual: Border cells glow when enemy is near
• Mechanics:
  - INTELLECT vs STEALTH contest
  - If INTELLECT > STEALTH: enemy revealed early
  - If STEALTH > INTELLECT: enemy remains hidden longer
  - First visual contact triggers border glow effect

PHASE 3: CONTACT (50-70% grid coverage)
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Active border cells meet
• Contested zone established
• Primary parameters: AGGRESSION, DEFENSE, VIRULENCE, RESILIENCE
• Visual: Noise texture mixing both virus colors, pulsing cells
• Mechanics:
  - Contested cells show mixed colors
  - AGGRESSION + VIRULENCE vs DEFENSE + RESILIENCE
  - Cells "wobble" between colors based on pressure
  - Higher pressure wins conversion

PHASE 4: DOMINATION (70-100% grid coverage)
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Winner emerges or stalemate declared
• LETHALITY triggers (kill vs convert decision)
• Visual: Cascade animation, rapid conversion
• Mechanics:
  - Losing virus cells rapidly convert
  - LETHALITY can instantly kill instead of convert
  - Victory cascade sweeps across grid

================================================================================
SECTION 4: PARAMETER MECHANICS (DETAILED)
================================================================================

AGGRESSION (⚔️)
───────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • +10% conversion speed per point in contested zones
  • Higher chance to win cell-to-cell combat
  • Formula: conversionChance = baseChance + (aggression × 0.1)

Visual Effect:
  • Red-tinged cell borders
  • Sharp angular growth patterns
  • Border glow intensity = aggression × contact pressure

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (AGG: 5) vs Virus B (AGG: 2)
  • Virus A converts contested cells 30% faster
  • Visual: Virus A cells have brighter red borders


MUTATION (🧬)
─────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • 5% chance per point to gain +1 random stat bonus in contested zones
  • Resistance to enemy conversion attempts
  • Can trigger "evolution event" - random parameter redistribution

Visual Effect:
  • Cells flicker through rainbow spectrum
  • Chromatic aberration shader on mutation events
  • Temporary color shifts

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (MUT: 4) has 20% chance to mutate
  • On mutation: gains +1 SPEED temporarily
  • Visual: Cell flashes purple, then pulses brighter


SPEED (⚡)
──────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Reduces tick interval between growth checks
  • Formula: tickInterval = 1000ms / (1 + speed × 0.1)
  • At SPEED 0: 1000ms per tick
  • At SPEED 10: 500ms per tick (2x faster)

Visual Effect:
  • Expansion animation plays faster
  • Cells "pulse" with energy
  • Animation speed multiplier = 1 + (speed × 0.05)

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (SPD: 8) vs Virus B (SPD: 2)
  • Virus A checks growth every 555ms
  • Virus B checks growth every 833ms
  • Virus A expands 50% faster


DEFENSE (🛡️)
────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Resistance to conversion - enemy needs higher local pressure
  • Formula: defenseMultiplier = 1 + (defense × 0.15)
  • At DEFENSE 10: enemy needs 2.5x pressure to convert

Visual Effect:
  • Cells have thicker borders
  • More saturated color
  • Border thickness shader parameter

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (DEF: 7) vs Virus B (AGG: 4)
  • Virus A resists conversion with 2.05x multiplier
  • Virus B needs 2x aggression to overcome


REPRODUCTION (🦠)
─────────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Base growth rate - determines empty→owned conversion speed
  • Formula: baseGrowth = 1 + (reproduction × 0.2)
  • At REPRODUCTION 10: 3x base growth rate

Visual Effect:
  • Cells appear "fuller"
  • Organic rounded shapes
  • Cell scale pulse animation frequency

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (REP: 9) vs Virus B (REP: 3)
  • Virus A converts empty cells 2.8x faster
  • Early game advantage for Virus A


STEALTH (👻)
────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Lower opacity to enemies
  • Enemy sees cells as "dormant" until contact duration > stealth value
  • Formula: revealDelay = stealth × 2 seconds
  • At STEALTH 10: 20 second delay before reveal

Visual Effect:
  • Desaturated color to enemies
  • Lower opacity (0.3-0.7 based on stealth)
  • Fades to normal opacity on reveal

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (STL: 8) vs Virus B (INT: 3)
  • Virus A remains hidden for 16 seconds
  • Virus B doesn't see Virus A expansion until late


VIRULENCE (☣️)
──────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Damage to enemy cell health on contact (not just conversion)
  • Formula: damagePerTick = virulence × 0.5
  • At VIRULENCE 10: 5 damage per tick

Visual Effect:
  • Cells have "spike" protrusions
  • Aggressive texture
  • Damage numbers float up on contact

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (VIR: 6) contacts Virus B
  • Virus B cells take 3 damage per tick
  • Visual: Purple spikes, floating damage numbers


RESILIENCE (💪)
───────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Recently lost cells easier to reclaim
  • Conversion resistance for X ticks after loss
  • Formula: reclaimBonus = resilience × 0.1 (for 10 ticks)
  • At RESILIENCE 8: +0.8 reclaim chance for 10 ticks

Visual Effect:
  • Cells "heal" visual damage
  • Resilient texture
  • Health bar overlay on cells

Combat Example:
  • Virus A loses cell, has RES: 7
  • For next 10 ticks: +0.7 chance to reclaim
  • Visual: Cell pulses green (healing)


MOBILITY (🚶)
─────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Cells can "slide" - position shifts within grid slot
  • Can reposition to reinforce weak fronts
  • Formula: moveRange = mobility × 1 cell
  • At MOBILITY 10: can move up to 10 cells

Visual Effect:
  • Cells not locked to grid centers
  • Smooth position interpolation
  • Movement trails

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (MOB: 5) detects weak flank
  • Cells slide 5 positions to reinforce
  • Visual: Cells glide to new positions


INTELLECT (🧠)
──────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Prioritizes expansion toward enemy weak points
  • Avoids strong defenses
  • Information access based on intellect level:
    - 0-2: See only territory colors
    - 3-5: See effect types (not causes)
    - 6-8: See enemy parameter categories
    - 9-12: See exact contested cell dominance values

Visual Effect:
  • Connection lines between cells
  • Network visualization
  • Bezier curves with data packets

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (INT: 10) vs Virus B (DEF: 8 on left, 2 on right)
  • Virus A sees weak right flank
  • Expands toward weak side, avoids strong left
  • Visual: Blue connection lines pulse toward weak point


CONTAGIOUSNESS (🫁)
───────────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • "Cloud" particles emanate from cells
  • Area-of-effect radius conversion
  • Formula: splashRadius = contagiousness × 1 cell
  • At CONTAGIOUSNESS 10: affects neighbors within 10 cells

Visual Effect:
  • Particle system for contagion clouds
  • Radial gradient overlays
  • Airborne "spores" float between cells

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (CON: 8) converts cell
  • All cells within 8 cells have 50% conversion chance
  • Visual: Purple cloud spreads from converted cell


LETHALITY (💀)
──────────────
Mechanical Effect:
  • Instantly kills low-health enemy cells instead of converting
  • Formula: killThreshold = lethality × 10 HP
  • At LETHALITY 10: kills cells with <100 HP

Visual Effect:
  • Dark cores in cells
  • "Deadly" aesthetic
  • Cell death animation (implosion)
  • Skull icon briefly appears

Combat Example:
  • Virus A (LETH: 9) contacts Virus B (HP: 50)
  • Virus B cell instantly killed (not converted)
  • Visual: Cell implodes, skull appears, fades to black

SECTION 5: THE CONTACT MECHANIC (BATTLEFIELD DYNAMICS)
================================================================================

THE CONTESTED ZONE SHADER
─────────────────────────
When two territories touch, a special shader region is created:

Visual Properties:
  • Noise texture mixing both player colors
  • Cells "wobble" between colors
  • Faster oscillation = more intense battle

Shader Uniforms (real-time combat data):
  • uPressureA = local AGGRESSION + VIRULENCE of player A
  • uPressureB = same for player B
  • uDominance = 0.0 to 1.0 (who is winning this specific cell)

Formula:
  pressureA = (aggA + virA) × cellCountA
  pressureB = (aggB + virB) × cellCountB
  dominance = pressureA / (pressureA + pressureB)

Example:
  • Virus A: AGG=5, VIR=3, 50 cells = 400 pressure
  • Virus B: AGG=7, VIR=2, 30 cells = 270 pressure
  • Dominance A = 400 / 670 = 0.597 (59.7%)
  • Visual: Cell is 60% red, 40% blue, wobbling


THE FOUR-PARAMETER COMBAT RESOLUTION
────────────────────────────────────
When cells are in contact, four parameters fight:

Attack Side (Aggressor):
  • AGGRESSION: Raw conversion power
  • VIRULENCE: Damage over time

Defense Side (Defender):
  • DEFENSE: Resistance multiplier
  • RESILIENCE: Recovery and reclaim chance

Resolution Formula:
  attackPower = aggression + virulence + (random 0-2)
  defensePower = (defense × 1.5) + resilience + (random 0-2)
  
  if attackPower > defensePower:
    cell converts to attacker
    damage = attackPower - defensePower
  else:
    cell remains defender
    reclaimChance = (defensePower - attackPower) × 0.1

Example Combat:
  • Attacker: AGG=6, VIR=4 = 10 attack power
  • Defender: DEF=5, RES=3 = 10.5 defense power
  • Defender wins! Cell remains, 5% reclaim chance

SECTION 6: PARAMETER SYNERGIES & BUILD ARCHETYPES
================================================================================

THE GLASS CANNON
────────────────
Build: AGG=5, VIR=4, LETH=3, DEF=0, RES=0
Strategy: Win contacts instantly, but loses territory fast if pushed back
Visual: Sharp red spikes, dark cores, thin borders
Risk: One bad contact = cascade loss
Counter: High DEFENSE + RESILIENCE ("The Turtle")


THE HIVE MIND
─────────────
Build: INT=4, REP=4, CON=4, MOB=0, LETH=0
Strategy: Networked cells share resources, expand efficiently, surround enemy
Visual: Connection lines everywhere, cloud particles, geometric patterns
Weakness: MOBILITY breaks network chains
Counter: High LETHALITY (kills cells before network forms)


THE GHOST
─────────
Build: STL=5, SPD=4, MUT=3, AGG=0, DEF=0
Strategy: Expand invisible, mutate on contact to counter enemy build
Visual: Faded colors, rainbow flickers, sudden reveals
Counter: High INTELLECT detects hidden cells earlier


THE TURTLE
──────────
Build: DEF=5, RES=4, DEF=3 (stacked), AGG=0, SPD=0
Strategy: Minimal expansion, impenetrable core, win by stalemate mutation
Visual: Thick borders, healing animations, compact shape
Risk: CONTAGIOUSNESS bypasses defenses


THE PLAGUE
──────────
Build: CON=5, REP=4, MOB=3, DEF=0, AGG=0
Strategy: Ignore direct contact, spread everywhere, choke enemy growth
Visual: Clouds cover map, cells slide constantly, chaotic borders
Counter: LETHALITY kills cells before they spread


THE SCHOLAR
───────────
Build: INT=5, MUT=4, RES=3, SPD=0, AGG=0
Strategy: Perfect information, adapts to enemy, outmaneuvers
Visual: Network visualization, data packets, calculated movements
Counter: Aggressive rush before intel gathers

SECTION 7: VICTORY CONDITIONS & ENDGAME
================================================================================

STANDARD VICTORY (80% Control)
──────────────────────────────
• Control 80% of grid for 5 consecutive seconds
• Cascade animation: remaining enemy cells rapidly convert
• Winner color sweeps across entire grid
• Duration: 5-10 seconds cascade

LETHALITY VICTORY (Extinction)
──────────────────────────────
• Kill (not convert) 50 enemy cells via LETHALITY parameter
• Special "extinction" animation
• Skull icons fill screen
• Black void spreads from killed cells

STALEMATE RESOLUTION (60 Second Timer)
──────────────────────────────────────
• If 60 seconds pass with neither above 60% control
• Mutation Event: Both players' parameters randomly redistributed
• Visual: Entire grid flashes white, new colors emerge
• Battle continues with new parameters

INTELLECT VICTORY (Rare - Assimilation)
───────────────────────────────────────
• If INTELLECT ≥ 10 and enemy has 0 INTELLECT
• "Assimilation": Enemy cells gradually switch without combat
• Visual: Enemy color desaturates, then shifts to your hue
• Duration: 10 seconds gradual shift

SECTION 8: VISUAL EFFECTS PRIORITY SYSTEM
================================================================================

EFFECT PRIORITY 1 (Always Visible):
  • Territory colors
  • Cell borders
  • Grid lines

EFFECT PRIORITY 2 (Visible on Hover):
  • Parameter values
  • Cell health bars
  • Conversion progress

EFFECT PRIORITY 3 (High-Energy Only):
  • Mutation rainbow flickers
  • Aggression spikes
  • Contagion clouds
  • Screen shake on major conversions

PARTICLE LIMITS:
  • Max 500 particles on screen
  • If exceeded: merge nearby into "cluster" sprites
  • Cluster shows number overlay (e.g., "×5")

================================================================================
SECTION 9: PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATION
================================================================================

TIER 1: DORMANT CELLS (90% of grid)
  • Rendered once to offscreen RenderTexture
  • Only redrawn if changed
  • PixiJS: Single large sprite with cached texture

TIER 2: ACTIVE CELLS (8% of grid)
  • Cells near territory edges
  • Updated every 5-10 frames
  • PixiJS: Container with individual sprites, batched

TIER 3: CONTESTED CELLS (2% of grid)
  • Active battle zones
  • Updated every frame with shader effects
  • PixiJS: Special container with custom filters

RULE: If total cell updates exceed 1000/frame, downgrade Tier 2 to 10-frame updates.
